Case File: Mr. Boran Wood

Alias: The Whispering Arborist
Age: 47
Occupation: Former forestry consultant, tree surgeon
Location: Pacific Northwest, USA
Status: Active fugitive, suspected in 9 confirmed killings over 12 years

Psychological Profile

  • Modus Operandi: Victims are found buried beneath rare trees, each chosen for symbolic meaning—grief, rebirth, silence.
  • Signature: A carved wooden token left in the victim’s mouth, etched with botanical Latin.
  • Psychology: Boran exhibits high-functioning sociopathy with obsessive eco-symbolism. He believes he’s “restoring balance” by punishing those who “defile nature.”
  • Trigger Event: A logging accident in 2009 killed his wife and daughter. He blames corporate greed and ecological ignorance.

Timeline of Events

  • 2011 “The Cedar Burial”: First known victim, a land developer found beneath a newly planted cedar.
  • 2014 “The Hemlock Pact”: Two hikers vanished; their remains discovered under a hemlock grove with a note: “They trampled sacred ground.”
  • 2017 “The Birch Confession”: A botanist who published a paper debunking tree sentience was found beneath a birch, her mouth filled with soil.
  • 2023 “The Redwood Silence”: A journalist investigating Boran’s past disappeared. Her camera was recovered, showing a blurred figure among redwoods.

Behavioral Patterns

  • Communication: Sends cryptic letters to environmental groups, quoting Thoreau and ancient forest myths.
  • Movement: Tracks seasonal migrations of birds and tree blooming cycles to time his killings.
  • Tools: Custom pruning blades, soil pH kits, and a drone modified for thermal tracking

Investigation Status

  • Current Leads: Surveillance footage from logging sites, DNA analysis of wooden tokens, and informants within eco-activist circles.
  • Challenges: Dense forest terrain, Boran’s intimate knowledge of wilderness survival, and his ability to blend into eco-communities.
  • Next Steps: Increase patrols in high-risk areas, collaborate with environmental organizations for intelligence, and deploy undercover agents at ecological events.

Detective Notes

    Weakness:
  • Boran avoids urban areas. He’s deeply ritualistic—any disruption to his “planting cycle” causes agitation.
    Suspect Interrogation Strategy:
  • Use symbolic language to provoke emotional response.
  • Reference his family loss subtly—he reacts violently to direct mention.
  • Present false ecological data to trigger correctional rants.
